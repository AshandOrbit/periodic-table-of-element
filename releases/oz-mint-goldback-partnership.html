Element United Partners with Oz Mint: Blockchain Authentication for Goldback Legal Tender

Utah-based mint accepts ELMT tokens, launches digital certificates of authenticity for Goldback legal tender notes

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Salt Lake City, Utah – November 2025

Element United today announced its partnership with Oz Mint, a Utah-based precious metals manufacturer now accepting ELMT tokens and launching digital certificates of authenticity for its Goldback legal tender notes through Element United's platform.

Every Goldback purchased from Oz Mint now comes with a verified digital certificate. Collectors and dealers benefit from permanent, immutable verification of provenance, authenticity, and ownership—whether for ultra-limited collectible notes or investment-grade legal tender.

Partnering with Oz Mint marks Element United's entry into precious metals authentication—the first of several planned expansions into luxury markets. Element United is positioning its NextHeir platform to serve high-value sectors, including fine watches, rare collectibles, and estate jewelry, with infrastructure built to scale globally.

"Gold and blockchain aren't competitors—they're companions," said Scott Lomu, President of Element United. "Element United's digital technology makes that relationship visible, accountable, and immutable."
"Oz Mint clients value tangible assets backed by centuries of trust," said Oz Mint. "Now they get something equally permanent in the digital realm—verified proof that what they hold is authentic, traceable, and theirs. Accepting ELMT and partnering with Element United brings modern transparency to timeless value."

Element United now provides enterprise-level authentication infrastructure for precious metals dealers, while Oz Mint customers receive both tangible assets and permanent digital proof of ownership.

About Oz Mint

Based in Utah, Oz Mint specializes in manufacturing high-quality investment-grade silver, gold, and copper rounds, as well as legal tender Goldback notes. Committed to customer satisfaction and the highest minting specifications, Oz Mint produces custom pieces that combine artistry, precious metals, and collectible value.
