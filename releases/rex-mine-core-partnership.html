Element United Brings CORE™ to U.S.A.: Rex Mine Partnership Announced

Arizona mining operation to digitize $1.4 trillion in-ground assets without extraction

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Phoenix, Arizona – November 2025

Element United today announced its first U.S. partnership with Rex Mine Gold (Arizona), bringing CORE™ into operation to digitize $1.4 trillion in verified in-ground assets without extraction.

Rex Mine represents Element United's first North American operation—proving that preservation can compete economically with mining.

"Rex Mine is proof that the 'Preserve, Don't Deplete' model isn't theoretical," said Scott Lomu, President of Element United. "It's operational."
"We're creating value without removing a single ounce," said the leadership of Rex Mine Gold. "Element United's verification systems transform our resources into economic assets while keeping them exactly where they are—in the ground, protected."

Visit ElementUnited.earth to discover how CORE™ can unlock your mine's in-ground value—or contact Element United for partnership opportunities.

CORE™ serves mining operations, landholders with mineral rights, and investors seeking verified asset valuation.

About Rex Mine Gold

Rex Mine Gold operates one of northern Arizona's most significant deposits, with $1.4 trillion in estimated in-ground assets. As Element United's first CORE™ partner in the United States, Rex Mine is digitizing its resources to generate economic value through preservation—setting new standards for how mines can contribute to U.S. national wealth without extraction.

About Element United

Element United provides blockchain-based verification systems spanning natural resource management, mining operations, and asset preservation. Through platforms like Identify.earth, STRIA™, CORE™, and NextHeir, Element United creates transparent, verifiable records that bridge industries—from ethical extraction to family heirlooms, from mine to market to memory.

Every element tells a story. Element United built the system that maps them all—making trust infrastructure.
