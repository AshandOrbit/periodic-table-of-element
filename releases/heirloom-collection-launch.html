Element United Unveils 'The Heirloom Digital Legacy Collection'

New blockchain-verified legacy preservation packages now available for pre-order; first 250 customers receive 20% off

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Salt Lake City, Utah – November 2025

Element United today unveiled the Heirloom Digital Legacy Collection—a new series of digital certificate packages designed for preserving personal and family heritage through blockchain verification.

Each tier offers a different scope of preservation, from single keepsakes to complete family archives:

• Keepsake Edition — One memory that matters
• Legacy Collection — A curated family collection
• Archive Collection — Build your digital heirloom archive
• Estate Collection — Preserve a generation with a complete vault

Each certificate includes verified provenance, optional appraisal integration, and the ability to upload documentation, photography, and stories. The collection marks the first product line in Element United's NextHeir platform, with expanded offerings including enhanced storage, storytelling and video support, custom templates, and one-on-one concierge services launching throughout Q1 2026.

"Your grandmother's ring. Your father's watch. They're not just objects—they're a testimony," said Scott Lomu, President of Element United. "The Heirloom Collection ensures that testimony endures."

Pre-order now at ElementUnited.earth to claim 20% off (first 250 customers).
